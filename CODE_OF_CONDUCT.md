# Code of Conduct

*Our shared commitment to a respectful community*

The Mars Society is committed to providing a welcoming, inclusive, and harassment-free experience for everyone — regardless of gender, gender identity, sexual orientation, disability, physical appearance, body size, race, ethnicity, age, religion, or technology choices.

## Our Standards

- **Be Respectful** — Treat all volunteers, staff, and community members with dignity and respect. Disagreements are natural; personal attacks are not.
- **Be Inclusive** — Welcome people of all backgrounds and experience levels. Help newcomers feel valued and supported.
- **Be Collaborative** — Share knowledge freely. Give and accept constructive feedback graciously. We're all working toward the same goal.
- **Be Professional** — Represent the Mars Society with integrity in all interactions, online and offline.
- **Be Accountable** — Own your mistakes, apologize to those affected, and learn from the experience.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination in any form
- Sexist, racist, homophobic, transphobic, or otherwise discriminatory language or imagery
- Personal attacks, trolling, or deliberately inflammatory comments
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Unwelcome sexual attention or advances
- Disrupting events, meetings, or collaborative work
- Any conduct that would be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Reporting & Enforcement

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it to the Mars Society. All reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly and confidentially.

Volunteers who violate this code of conduct may face consequences including:

- A private warning and conversation
- Temporary suspension from volunteer activities
- Permanent removal from the volunteer program

## Our Mission Comes First

We are united by a shared vision: advancing the human exploration and settlement of Mars. This code of conduct exists to protect that mission and the community that drives it. By volunteering with the Mars Society, you agree to uphold these standards.
